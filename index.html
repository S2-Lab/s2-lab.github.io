S2Lab (Secure Software Lab)

Our lab is dedicated to software and systems security problems. In particular, our research focus is designing and implementing advanced bug sanitization and mitigation techniques to protect operating systems and user applications in existing and emerging areas(e.g., autonomous driving, drones) by leveraging compiler-based, fuzzing, and AI technologies
